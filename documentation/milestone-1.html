Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience and Goal

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to showcase my web development abilities and to help the family business gain more traction online.

Audience

The primary target audience of this website will be new and returning customers who are in need of plumbing services who are located in the Silver City area of southern New Mexico. These customers will vary widely as plumbing and HVAC services are something that everyone is in need of at one point or another. The business offers a wide variety of services from smaller things like leaky faucets to full remodels, water heater installations or HVAC system installation and service. The business can also assist with utility issues such as water mains, sewer mains, gas service lines, and an occasional septic system. Additional clients that may be attracted to the business would be commercial clients.

The secondary audience I would like to reach would be potential employers or internships, or even possible freelance clientele. I would like to provide this website as a good example of my skills and the hard work that I put into every project I take on.

Goal

There are two primary objectives I would like to achieve with this project:

  1. Cultivating my skills and experience in developing different projects for my portfolio

    2. I would like to work on several different projects and different types of projects to get as much practice using front end and back end development skills as I'm able to. I would like to add this project to my professional portfolio once this bootcamp comes to a close as a central piece of work since it will be completely designed and programmed by me, and me, alone. I think this will be a great way to get in practice for the entire process of beginning a new project as well as practicing the skills I have learned thus far in this bootcamp.

  2. Assisting that family business that my grandparents started decades ago to gain an online presence and provide additional information for potential customers

    3. During the COVID-19 pandemic, it became very clear that small businesses that do not have an online presence are at a significant disadvantage over other competitors who do have an online presence. I feel very passionate about giving these small or local businesses a fighting chance to be seen by a wider audience and I would like to eventually start my own small business offering this service. Because of that, I wanted to start with a business that I know doesn't have any kind of online presence outside a Facebook page that is rarely updated if ever. I would like to help my family gain more visibility even if they are fairly well known locally. In addition to helping expand their customer base, I would also like to save them valuable time by helping customers get the answers to frequently asked questions without having to contact the business via phone to do so.

Persona

Name: Naomi McLeod

Profile

  • Naomi is a full-time nurse who works 12 hours a day.
  • She doesn't have a lot of free time outside of work aside from the time she spends resting and recovering from the long days.
  • Recently, her water heater at home stopped working and needs a replacement .She has been taking showers at the hospital as a temporary solution, but she needs a new one installed immediately.
  • Naomi's schedule varies from week to week and she's only aware of her schedule two weeks in advance.
  • She doesn't have a computer at home, so she is only able to use her phone, which is an android note S20 that she uses to do research on available options for her needs.
  • If the information she finds doesn't mention anything about emergency services or the process, she will likely go somewhere else.

Use Case

I am a light user who needs to get a water heater replacement immediately

Interaction Flow

Precondition:

Naomi searches Google to find local plumbing and installation services and clicks on the link directing her to J & S Plumbing and Heating.

Postcondition:

Naomi finds the information she is looking for and is able to get the process started to get a replacement water heater.

Interaction Flow

Upon opening the website, Naomi sees a link in the top navigation bar that says services and clicks on it.

The website takes her to the services section of the website.

Naomi finds the link that says 'Water Heaters' and clicks on it.

A dialogue box beneath the link expands to give more detailed information about water heaters, maintenance, replacement, and installation.

Naomi clicks the link that says contact us.

The website takes her to the contact form, which also contains location information and telephone number.

Naomi clicks into each field to fill out her information and a message stating what she is looking for and clicks submit.

The website displays a modal that says her request has been received and when to expect a response.
