Milestone Two

Content Strategy

Header

The header will contain the logo and name of the business on the left of the uppermost banner. The navigation bar will overlap with the banner and the hero image to provide a two-dimensional feel to the website. On the bottom of the hero image will be 4 containers that contain quick and easy access to what the business is about - primarily plumbing, hvac, and utility services with an image accompanying the description. These containers will also overlap with the hero image.

About Us

This section will focus on information about the business with a focus on being a family owned and operated small business. This section will also contain information about how long they have been in business and servicing the area, some information about their mission, as well as an image meant to draw users in, either of the business, work they have done, or the current owner. It may also contain an excerpt from a magazine article the business was featured in or a photo from the same source.

Our Services

This section will consist of six images that show or describe the main services the company is hired for most often, as well as custom services that are only offered by the company. Initially, I would like to have the image dimmed to allow the text of the service name to be more of a focus and more readable. When a user hovers over it, the text will dim or be removed completely and the image will no longer be dimmed. Additionally, a flyout text box will appear beneath the image to provide more detailed information about that service.

Testimonials

The testimonial section will contain business reviews from real customers that are pulled from Google reviews and contain a review title, reviewer name, a check mark icon if the review is verified, a star rating, image if one is available, and the content of the review. Each review will be in a container that behaves like a slideshow to allow users to focus on one review at a time instead of having so many things to read on the page. This will draw their focus and not overwhelm users.

Contact Us

This section will contain a simple contact form. On the left side will be a standard form to fill out name, email, phone number and a message. Underneath the text box for the message is a checkbox that users can check if they are in need of emergency services. I plan to include a modal that will pop up when that box is checked to advise the user that the best way to contact the business for immediate service is to contact them by phone or stop into the office. On the right side of the contact sections will be a image or link to Google maps to find the location of the office. Below that is a listing of the business name, location, phone number, and email address.

Footer

Because this is a simple one page website, there's not a need for an elaborate site map in the footer, which is why I plan to keep it simple. The links provided in the footer will be the same links that are available in the top navigation bar for easy access back to the top or a specific section. The only other link that will be provided is a link to the Facebook page on the right corner of the footer. On the left corner of the footer will be an image of the company logo to enhance branding.
